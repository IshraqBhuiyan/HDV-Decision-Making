A project by Chia Chen, Ishraq Bhuiyan, Fangzhou Yu, Sean Fang and Gordon Wang

Your name is Ronald McDonald and you are a pilot. Today you are the Captain of Disunited Airlines Flight 91 from New York to Florida.

Here is the story of one fateful day for you.

Next
Heavy snow temporarily closed the airport, but now it's finally open again. I really want to get out of this frozen hell and be back under the warm Florida Sun. I haven't seen my family in so long too. I'd really like to get back as fast as possible. Well, it's time to go to my airplane.
I arrive in the cockpit of the Boeing 737 assigned to you with my copilot.
I begin the preflight checks. Me: Engines?
Copilot: Check.
Me: Fuel?
Copilot: Check.
Me: Tire Pressure?
Copilot: Check.

Have they deiced the plane yet? It seems that the airport deicing crew is tending to it right now. It should be done within 15 to 20 minutes.

The weather outside is gradually worsening. If we don't take off soon, we may get grounded again. I really want to get back to Florida and have some time with my family before my next flight. I rarely see them nowadays. The passengers are also extremely tired and anxious. I don't want to be the one telling them we can't take off and then listen to their complaints.
